Socialblood is building the largest network of blood donors, Hospitals and Blood banks on the web.

Socialblood was founded by Karthik Naralasetty in 2011. He dropped out of Rutgers University, New Jersey to create his own tech-company and has been an Internet Entrepreneur ever since. In June 2011, he noticed the structural deficiencies in the local system of blood transfusions when he witnessed a family’s struggle to find a regular transfusion for their four year old to treat her Thalassemia; a disease which affects around 40 million Indians.

At Socialblood, the mission is not only to create an easy, trustworthy and a reliable network of donors around the world but also grow together with Hospitals, Blood-banks and supporting organizations.We aim to change and disrupt the archaic ways of finding blood donors and build the largest network of voluntary donors around the world. Making it as easy as a few clicks.
